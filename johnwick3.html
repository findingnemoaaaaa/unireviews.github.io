He is pursued by various gangs of assassins and killed by the Director, head of the Ruska Roma crime syndicate. John meets Sofia, his former friend and manager of the Moroccan Continental, and demands to be directed to the Elder, the only person above the High Table. John rescues Sofia's daughter and tries to find the Elder, but Sofia's dog is killed by a bulletproof vest. John collapses in the desert and is brought to The Elder, where he agrees to forgive John if he kills Winston and remains subservient to the High Table for the remainder of his life.

John returns to New York and is attacked by Zero and his students before reaching the Continental's protection. The Adjudicator arrives, but John refuses to abdicate and John refuses to kill him. The Adjudicator revokes the Continental's neutral status and sends Zero and an army of heavily armed High Table enforcers to kill John and Winston. John is ambushed by Zero and his students, but is eventually defeated and left to die. On the roof of the Continental, the Adjudicator agrees to a parley with Winston, who offers allegiance to the High Table. John arrives, is shot, and John is secretly delivered to the Bowery King in an underground bunker.
