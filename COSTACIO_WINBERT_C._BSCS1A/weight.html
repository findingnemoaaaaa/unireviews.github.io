Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-joo


Kim Bok-Joo, a promising collegiate female weightlifter, is a member of a team that is not popular with the boys and has no boyfriends. Meanwhile, Jung Joon-Hyung, a 21-year-old collegiate swimmer, is a free spirit raised by his uncle and aunt. One day, Kim bumps into Jung Joon-Hyung while riding his bicycle. Kim picks up a handkerchief left behind by Jung, which is important to him. After cleaning and ironing it, Kim goes to the swimming pool to give back the handkerchief.

Kim picks up a handkerchief left behind by Jung Joon-Hyung and finds it important to him. She cleans and irons the handkerchief, then gives it back to Jung Joon-Hyung. When he sees Kim Bok-Joo cleaning it, he explodes with anger when he sees she cleaned it. Kim Bok-Joo falls into the swimming pool, and Jung Joon-Hyung jumps to save her. At that moment, Kim Bok-Joo and Jung Joon-Hyung realize they have seen each other before, as they saved each other's life when they were both children.

Comment:

"Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok-joo" excels in its relatable characters, focusing on Kim Bok-joo, a talented weightlifter with dreams of becoming a gold medalist. The drama's romance between Bok-joo and swimmer Jung Joon-hyung is sweet, innocent, and genuine, capturing the excitement, confusion, and tenderness of young love.
