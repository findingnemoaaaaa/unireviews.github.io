In Reborn!, Tsunayoshi "Tsuna" Sawada is chosen to become the tenth boss of the Vongola Family, as he is the great-great-great grandson of the first Vongola boss. Reborn, an infant hitman from Italy, trains Tsuna using the "Dying Will Bullet" method, which causes a person to be "reborn" with a stronger self to execute their dying wish. Tsuna becomes stronger, more confident, and willing, making him a suitable Vongola family boss despite his reluctance. He makes friends, including his love interest Kyoko Sasagawa.

Tsuna faces various challenges on his way to becoming the Vongola boss, including fighting escaped Mafia convicts and the Varia, who want their boss, Xanxus, to be the Vongola boss. To defeat the Varia, Tsuna and his friends are transported to the future to face the Millefiore family, who are killing the Vongolas. They discover that the Arcobaleno, the seven strongest infants, are dead except for Lal Mirch. Tsuna and his group defeat the Millefiore and the Simon Family, who have sworn revenge on the Vongola founding father for allegedly betraying the first Simon boss.

After reconciling with Simon, Reborn and other Arcobaleno compete to remove their curse. The Vindice enter the competition, and Tsuna defeats Bermuda and his team. Checker Face, who inflicted the Arcobaleno Curse of the Rainbow, reveals his true identity as Kawahira, administrator of the humankind's ultimate power, Tri-ni-set.

Comment:

"Hitman Reborn!" is a one-of-a-kind combination of genres, beginning with a comical slice-of-life narrative about school life and surreal comedy. The storyline evolves into an action-oriented shonen fight series as the plot unfolds, highlighting Tsuna and his comrades' growth and evolution against formidable foes. The action-packed fight sequences in the series are aesthetically beautiful and intriguing.
