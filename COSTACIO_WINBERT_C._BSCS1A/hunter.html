Gon Freecss, a young Hunter, learns from Kite that his parents are dead and decides to become one. He leaves Whale Island to take the Hunter Examination, where he meets Kurapika, Leorio, and Killua. Gon encounters Hisoka, a mysterious transmuter, and passes the exam, except for Killua, who fails due to his brother Illumi's influence. They later meet Zushi, a fellow Heavens Arena applicant, and play Greed Island, a rare and expensive video game with Nen-like properties. They train their Nen and learn to shape their abilities to their traits.

Gon and Killua help Kite research a Chimera Ant queen, who is a man-sized insect that devours other creatures and gives birth to progeny that inherit their characteristics. They face the Royal Guard of the Chimera Ants and the Hunter Association, who send a team of powerful Hunters to defeat them. Netero, the president of the Hunter Association, is killed in combat, and Gon has a showdown with Neferpitou. The Hunter Association's top echelons, the Zodiacs, decide to choose Netero's replacement as Chairman. Netero's son Beyond assembles an expedition to the Dark Continent, sponsored by the Kingdom of Kakin.

Chrollo regains his powers and fulfills Hisoka's wish, but Hisoka is defeated and killed. Kurapika, who infiltrates the ship, aims to retrieve the last batch of scarlet eyes from the Kurta Clan.
