I'm Not a Robot


Kim Min-kyu, chairman and largest shareholder of KM Financial, suffers from severe allergies to human contact and develops life-threatening rashes. His ex-girlfriend Ji-ah, who is trying to create businesses based on her inventions, is also involved in the Santa Maria team. Professor Hong Baek-gyun's team has secretly created an advanced humanoid robot called AG3 (pronounced Aji 3), modeled after his ex-girlfriend Ji-ah. Min-kyu and his father try to sell the Santa Maria team to Bold Group, owned by foreign investor Martin. Baek-gyun sends the robot to him as a demonstration, but the robot short-circuits and Baek-gyun asks Ji-ah to pose as the robot.

Ji-ah impersonates the robot, and Min-kyu falls in love with her, believing she is Aji 3. He reboots her, erasing the robot's memory, and replaces her with the real Aji 3. Min-kyu discovers Ji-ah's deception and has a violent allergic reaction that nearly kills him. Baek-gyun saves him, and Min-kyu and Ji-ah rediscover their love as humans. Aji 3 stores all video footage and data from the trials with Min-kyu in a secret cache, which is hacked to reveal Min-kyu's illness.

Min-kyu manages to thwart the attempt, uncovering evidence of Hwang Do-won and Martin's involvement in the murder of Chairman Park. The Santa Maria team successfully launches a new robot after changing its appearance, and they, along with Ji-ah and Min-kyu, live happily ever after.

Comment:

"I'm Not a Robot" features Kim Min-kyu, a wealthy businessman with a severe allergy to humans, and Jo Ji-ah, a woman pretending to be a robot to help him overcome his condition. The show explores themes of trust, empathy, and human connection, with Yoo Seung-ho portraying a socially isolated and emotionally guarded character and Chae Soo-bin bringing charm and warmth to their on-screen dynamic.
