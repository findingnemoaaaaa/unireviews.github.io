Dr. Stone


In 2019, Senku Ishigami is revived to a world where human civilization has been eroded for 3,700 years. He sets up a base camp and studies the petrified humans to determine the cause and cure. Senku's friend Taiju Oki discovers nitric acid's ability to revive others, starting with high school martial artist Tsukasa Shishio and Yuzuriha Ogawa. They aim to rebuild civilization with a focus on science. Tsukasa opposes Senku's idea of forming a new scientific civilization and destroys petrified adults to prevent interference.

After recovering, Senku discovers a tribe of people who see the opportunity to create his Kingdom of Science. They learn the benefits of science for survival and become more trusted by the tribe. Senku's Kingdom of Science engages in a war with the Tsukasa Empire, emerging victorious. They discover that Senku's adopted father left precious metals for mass-producing revival fluid on an island inhabited by the Petrification Kingdom, who possess the device used to petrify the world.
