Naruto Shippuden


The Ninja World is a world where powerful foxes attack Konoha, a hidden leaf village in the Land of Fire. Minato Namikaze seals the fox inside his son, Naruto Uzumaki, making him a host of the beast. Naruto's father's life is lost, and the Third Hokage returns to lead Konoha. After 12 years, Naruto learns about the Nine-Tails and joins Team 7, led by Kakashi Hatake. They complete various missions, including a major one in the Land of Waves.

Kakashi allows Team 7 to take a ninja exam, allowing them to advance to higher ranks and take more challenging missions. Orochimaru, a wanted criminal, invades Konoha and kills the Third Hokage. Jiraiya, a legendary ninja, decides to train Sasuke due to his Sharingan genetic heritage. Sasuke joins Orochimaru to gain the strength needed to kill Itachi. Naruto and Sakura continue their quest, training with Jiraiya and Sakura as Tsunade's apprentice.

Comment:

"Naruto" has a wide group of characters, including Naruto, a motivated protagonist who progresses from a naughty troublemaker to a skillful ninja. The supporting characters, Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno, each have their own backstory and motives, showing Naruto's development and the value of friendship and devotion.
